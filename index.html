About me

I'm sure there are many descriptors you could use to accurately describe me. I like to think these are my top picks: family man, forever learner, challenge conqueror, and efficiency seeker.

First and foremost, I got into this career field in order to work from and spend more time with family. This field has offered just that and has been an amazing blessing. Secondly, I love to learn. Currently learning Korean on top of further developing myself technically. Everyday you choose not to learn is a day you step backwards.

Next comes the challenge conquering and efficiency seeking. This field is anything but a breeze and that is what I love about the work I do. Each new task is a challenge to face, learn, and overcome. Maintaining proper documentation then helps to improve efficency in the long run as others come to work behind you or on similar tasks.

If the quick little tid bit about me here didn't satiate your palate, please click the link and join me for a deeper look into who I am, what brought me here, and the things I enjoy. Hope to see you there!
